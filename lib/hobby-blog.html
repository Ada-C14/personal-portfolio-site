My Secret Life Advice for Everyone: Do Improv

Improv makes you think on your feet.

It impowers you to go "I'm not enjoying this scene, I'm just gonna nope on out" or "I no longer like what I'm doing, I'm going to do something entirely different until I do feel good about what I'm doing."

Improv teaches you to have a stage ego - to believe that you deserve to take up space.
