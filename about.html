Mackenzie was born and raised in the Pacific Northwest. She grew up in Des Moines, Washington and graduated from the University of Washington, with a B.S. in Public Health. She has passions for women's health and fertility, organization, pugs, making blended tropical drinks, genetics, Italian food, metalworking, and coding.

She has worked in finance and administration in higher ed, is a licensed phlebotomist, worked in third party reproduction (gestational carriers and egg, sperm, and embryo donation) at a fertility clinic, did minor web design, barbacked at an Aussie bar, staged homes, nannied, been an elementary school TA and mentor, performed community public health outreach, and has finally found a field that combines her interests and skills.

She is a part of Ada's C14 cohort and loves that coding combines so many elements of her previous experiences, and advanced her interests farther.
