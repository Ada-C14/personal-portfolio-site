who is pauline?

pauline chane is idaho-born and seattle-raised, and supposedly part hakka (she is definitely taiwanese-chinese american, however). in senior year, she gave up a pursuit of a math career to get a degree combining her two worst high school subjects: chemistry and biology.

while she did learn to love math AND both chemistry and biology, she also learned to not really like most software used in the lab. pauline hopes to change that one day through her newest love of code.
