Travel

Alice has love for trips, she believes that the end of a trip is just the beginning of another one, it is necessary to see what has not been seen, to see in the spring what has been seen in the summer, to see what has been seen at night, to see with the sun where the rain first fell, see the harvest green, the ripe fruit, a stone that has moved, a shadow that was not here. It is necessary to go back to the steps that have been taken, to repeat and to trace new paths beside them. You have to restart the trip. Travel to see, smell, touch, hear and taste mountains and valleys, forests and mountains, rivers and sand dunes, walk in the desert or climb a snow-covered hill, enjoying stones, flowers and animals, savoring without destroying.
