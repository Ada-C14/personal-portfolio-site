Pumpkim Shrimp

  • 1 large pumpkim
  • 1 large pumpkim
  • 8 chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 ½ chopped onion
  • salt, black pepper
  • lemon juice
  • 3 tbs olive oil
  • 1kg fresh shrimp
  • 2 chooped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of tomato paste
  • 1/4 bunch of cilantro
  • 1 chili peppers(optional)
  • 1 can of milk or table cream
  • 1 cup of cream cheese
  1. Open the pumpkin, remove the seeds. Reserve.
  2. Mix 5 garlic cloves, with ½ onion, black pepper, salt and oil.
  3. Rub this paste inside the pumpkin, cover and bake at 180 degrees for 40 minutes.
  4. Season the prawns with salt, pepper and lemon juce. In a hot pan, add olive oil, brown the prawns, and set aside
  5. Sauté the remaining onion, garlic and add the tomatoes and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the cilantro, pepper, milk cream, cream cheese, shrimp and correct the salt.
  7. Cover with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes or until browned

Chocolate Pumpkim Cream

  • 2 dates
  • 1 cup roasted pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup natural Greek yogurt
  • 125 g melted dark chocolate
  • 1 tbsp Cocoa Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • a pinch of salt, nutmeg and cloves
  1. Put all the ingredients in a food processor and grind until you get a homogeneous and silky mixture.
  2. Place the cream in two larger, greedy glasses or in four smaller portions.
  3. Cover the bowls or cups with cling film and chill.
  4. Serve with a few spoonfuls of Greek yogurt, crushed cookies and chopped chocolate.
