(More) About Me

I studied chemical engineering at the University of Utah. While in school I used Matlab and Python for data visualization, optimization, and simulating process control. After graduating I worked in IP law.

I moved to Seattle in 2019, with my partner and our two dogs. Currently working on becoming a full stack developer at Ada Developers Academy. I love learning new things and want to work on both client-side and server-side software. 

My favorite podcasts include (not ordered) Planet Money, This American Life, and Freakonomics. I make kombucha and sourdough (from kombucha).
